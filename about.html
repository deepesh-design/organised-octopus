About

Your home called. It wants art.

Like the octopus, we're intelligent, adaptive and endlessly resourceful — reaching into every corner of your home with furniture built to last.

Values

Art in utility

Every piece is a sculptural object first — function follows form, but never at its expense.

Routine as ritual

We don't sell furniture. We sell the architecture of the rituals that happen around it.

Designer

Kei Rose

Kei designs every piece in the collection — Luno, Arco and Vera each a study in how Swiss structural order can furnish a room.
